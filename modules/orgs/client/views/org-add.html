Register Your Collaborator on the City Opportunity Platform (Beta)
Registering your collaborator means that any site viewer will be able to see:
  • Your collaborator name
  • A link to your website if you choose to share it

* The individual members you associate to your collaborator profile will NOT be seen by site viewers; they will only be seen by the issuer of a government opportunity to which you've put those members forward in a Proposal.
